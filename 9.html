All the Important Details

How can I order custom school or club items?

Pick a template, send logos/colors, approve visuals. Mixed sizing and bulk shipping supported.

What makes Vitalini outerwear different?

How do sizes work for team orders?

Youth + adult size runs; fit kits available on request. One order, full roster.

Do you sell individual pieces?

Yes—select pieces available individually depending on stock/season.

Tracked EU/UK/CH/US shipping; duties handled per destination.

Typical lead time 4–6 weeks. Defects covered; paid repairs for heavy wear.
